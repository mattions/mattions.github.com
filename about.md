<p>I'm Michele Mattioni — a researcher and developer working at the intersection of <strong>computational neuroscience</strong> and <strong>open source software</strong>.</p>

<p>My work focuses on building tools and models that help us understand the brain. I've developed 3D neuron visualization tools, multiscale simulation frameworks, and contributed to open standards in neuroscience (NeuroML, SBML).</p>

<h2>What I care about</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Open science</strong> — reproducible research, open data, open standards</li>
  <li><strong>Computational neuroscience</strong> — modeling neurons, synapses, and circuits</li>
  <li><strong>Sustainability</strong> — technology that serves society and the planet</li>
</ul>
