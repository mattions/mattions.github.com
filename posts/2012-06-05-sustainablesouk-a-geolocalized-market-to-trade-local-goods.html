SustainableSouk, a geolocalized market to trade local goods

2012-06-05local foodlocal goodsgeospatialmaps

The SustainableSouk was an attempt to create a geolocalized sustainable system, where super local producers (urban growers,small farmers) can find super busy customers, like students, working people.

The project was set up with the collaboration of Transition Cambridge and it was concluded in 2015.
